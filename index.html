Methodology
Ensemble Model — auto-selects the best algorithm per metric from: (1) OLS Linear Regression (straight-line trends), (2) Polynomial Regression degree-2 (acceleration/deceleration), (3) Holt Exponential Smoothing with auto-tuned α/β (weights recent months more heavily). Winner chosen by lowest MAPE on last 3 months. Seasonal adjustment applied when ≥6 months history available. Confidence bands = ±1 std dev of residuals. Partial month excluded. These are statistical signals — treat alongside operational pipeline context.
